Design Principles

Proximity

  • Company Name: Petrobras S.A.
  • Web Site: Petrobras S.A
  • Description:

    The proximity principle is move elements closer together or farther apart. The elements that have similar features can be grouped as the subtitles, authors, address ... creating connections.

    It is observed that the textual elements were grouped, drawing attention to the main points of the page.

Alignment

  • Company Name: Locaweb Ltda.
  • Web Site: Locaweb Ltda
  • Description:

    The alignment principle is how elements are placed on page. Place the elements to the center, right or left, on top or bottom, with the purpose of establishing a page organization improving the visual appearance or layout of the page,

    The alignments on this page are centered using the small screen of the mobile. The use of columns aligned with their centralized content separates the informations. The use of centralized alignment is primary, however in this case, in my opinion it is being used well.

Contrast

  • Company Name: Samsung
  • Web Site: Samsung
  • Description:

    The contrast principle is make a drawing that remains in your memories only when you see it. The contrast can be done with color, font, shape, etc. helping in the usability of a composition.

    On this page we can see the application of the contrast in the titles, in the image with the diagonal text, and in the secondary texts with less emphasis. Not much color was used to elaborate the contrast, but the gradient and leftover image of the phone stand out on the blank page, calling attention to the product they sell.

Repetition

  • Company Name: Dell Technogies
  • Web Site: Dell
  • Description:

    The reptition principle is repeat an element more than once in a design. The repetition creates a feeling of organized movement and consistency in the composition. It can also help in the business tactic, solidifying, for example, brand identity.

    Dell used the principle of repetition in the standard formats of its buttons and in the formatting of its categories, helping the user to quickly identify with his vision what is of interest.
