South Fork Lodge

Winding through 65 miles of waterfalls, lush valleys, and steep canyons, the South Fork of Idaho’s Snake River offers Blue Ribbon fly-fishing on scenic waters chock-full of Yellowstone cutthroat and rainbow trout. The South Fork Lodge offers luxury accommodations directly on the river with access of some of the world’s best fly-fishing guides. Choose from charming cabins, deluxe lodge suites, or a private, 4-bedroom cabin with timber beams and glass walls. All accommodations feature modern amenities, high-end decor, outdoor space, and river views. For a truly seamless experience, inquire about our all-inclusive packages, which include daily guided fly-fishing, locally sourced and inspired breakfast, lunch and dinner at the Main Lodge, nightly drinks until 10 p.m., food, beverage, and gratuities. South Fork Lodge outfitters and guides will ensure an unforgettable time on the river, no matter your fly-fishing experience. Our reservations agents can also help you plan memorable events on the banks of the Snake River, including corporate retreats, reunions and weddings. Whatever your reason to gather, ask us how we can make your special day extraordinary. South Fork Lodge is a permittee of Caribou-Targhee National Forest.
