The Salmon River

Salmon Raft of McCall, Idaho invites you to come along for an unforgettable whitewater rafting adventure. We are proud to be based in beautiful downtown McCall and provide transportation to the river and back to home base. The Salmon River boasts legendary crystal clear waters, spacious white sand beaches, and is the second deepest canyon gorge in North America. Perhaps more luring, however, is the fact that the Salmon River is the longest free-flowing river in the lower 48 states. Choose from one of our single day adventures for a quick getaway, our two and three-day excursions for a longer wilderness experience, or jump right in with our team on a longer four or five-day journey through the remote Lower Salmon River Canyons. Our team of Salmon River rafting expert guides will ensure your experience is top-notch and unforgettable. Read some our frequently asked questions and about the Salmon Raft difference.
